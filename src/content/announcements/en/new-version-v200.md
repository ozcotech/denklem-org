The new version (v2.0.0) of the Denklem mobile application is coming very soon. In this release, the 2026 tariff is added alongside the 2025 tariff, allowing you to make calculations with more up-to-date data.

The new version will be released with all its new features within January 2026. A new interface, new icon, new design, liquid glass style, faster and sharper calculations will all be available in this new version. Please follow updates for your app.
