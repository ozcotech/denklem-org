Denklem is a mobile application developed for fast and accurate calculation of mediation fees. In this guide, we cover the basic usage steps.

## Step 1: Select the Tariff Year

When the app opens, the current tariff year is automatically selected. You can change the year selection if you want to calculate based on past tariffs.

## Step 2: Determine the Dispute Type

For monetary disputes, use the **Monetary Dispute** option. Employment law, rent, commercial, and other dispute types fall under this category.

## Step 3: Select Agreement Status

- **Agreement:** If the parties have agreed, enter the agreed amount.
- **No Agreement:** Calculate by entering the number of parties.

## Step 4: Review the Result

The calculation result is displayed along with the mediation fee and, if necessary, Freelance Invoice (SMM) information. You can share or print the result.

## Time and SMM Calculation

You can also access the **Time Calculation** and **SMM Calculation** modules from the main screen. Time calculation automatically computes mediation process durations.
