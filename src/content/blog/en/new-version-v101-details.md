The v1.0.1 update of the Denklem mobile application has been released. This version was prepared based on feedback received since the first release.

## Improvements Made

**Performance improvements:** Calculation speed has been optimized. Bracket calculations for large amounts in particular work faster.

**Minor interface fixes:** Text alignment and visual inconsistencies on some screens have been resolved.

**Stability:** Rare crashes and freezes have been fixed.

## How Do I Update?

Open the App Store, go to the Updates tab and press the "Update" button for Denklem. Or you can update directly by visiting the app page.

We always look forward to your feedback.
